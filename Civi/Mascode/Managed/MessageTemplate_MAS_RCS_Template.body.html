These two forms (RCS and SAS) will give our Volunteer Consultants more information to help them decide if they would be right for your project.

Please note we post all available projects for our Volunteer Consultants on a weekly basis and it may take up to a month for a project to be picked up.  Once a Volunteer Consultant indicates interest we will refer them to you for a ‘meet n greet’ and to allow you both to determine if and how you may proceed with the work.  Please note our Volunteer Consultants provide analysis, advice and guidance but do not generally engage in hands on work.  Please don’t hesitate to call me if you would like further detail on any element of this process.

MAS is a free service. We ask if our Volunteer Consultants have travel or other expenses that the agency reimburse them on receipt, and, if you are pleased with the service and can afford to, that the agency consider a donation to MAS as we are a registered charity ourselves and this will help with overhead. Unfortunately as we have no staff resources we cannot and do not respond to RFPs. Typically MAS is working for organizations that cannot afford to engage paid consultants.
